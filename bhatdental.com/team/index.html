Team

Narayana Bhat   BDS MDS DMD FAGD
Dr Bhat obtained his DMD from Tufts University School of Dental Medicine, Boston, Massachusetts. While in dental school Dr Bhat was involved in multiple organizations , both academic and community service related. Dr Bhat was also conferred the FAGD by the Academy of General Dentistry. In addition Dr Bhat has had extensive training and experience from studying and working abroad in India and Dubai. Dr Bhat continues to be involved in his local community and is an active member of the Eagles Landing Community Master Association. Professionally, he is an active member of the American dental association, Georgia Dental Association, Academy of General dentistry and the Hinman Dental Committee. Being involved in these organisations coupled with numerous Continuing Education programs help Dr Bhat to be in sync with the latest trends in dentistry. Dr Bhat AKA "Batman" Ioves to run outdoors and has successfully completed the Boston Marathon four times. He is a regular participant of our own Peachtree Road Race for many years now. Dr Bhat enjoys spending time with his lovely family and his large circle of friends. Movies are a passion when he finds free time !

Megha Hegde-Bhat   BDS FDSRCS(Eng) FDSRCS(Edin) DMD
Dr Hegde-Bhat (AKA Dr Megha to her patients) graduated from Tufts University School of Dental Medicine,Boston, Massachusetts with a Summa Cum Laude where she was also elected to the Omicron Kappa Upsilon Honor Society. Dr Hegde-Bhat has had extensive education and work experience in the United States and abroad in India , Scotland and Dubai. She continues to keep herself updated and advanced with the current trends and technology by attending numerous continuing education classes. Dr Hegde-Bhat enjoys being a homemaker and loves to spend time with her family and friends. She is also a regular at the Peachtree Road race for many years now.
